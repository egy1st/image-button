InitForm

Prepare form to know every thing about the form and its buttons , so it must be the first assignment in your code. an error may occurs if you do not.

syntax:

Function InitForm(ByRef dm_Form As System.Windows.Forms.Form)

dm_Form is a reference to your form which passed as 'Me' keyword


Example:

Dim IB As New DynamicComponents.ImageButton()
IB.InitForm(Me)
